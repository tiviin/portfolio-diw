Descrição

Fairy Brawlers - Introduction The world of fairy tales is no longer the same! The kings and queens have become emperors and ampresses and now the regional oligarchies are making the creatures of the magic world, neverland and wonderland bear the expenses and bills of their homes! Because of this, the great and merciful emperor of the magic world, Beast (yes, he wanted to keep the Beast name) created a way to benefit the best warrior in the fantasy world, with his annual festival: “Fairy Brawlers”, fighting competition in which the last one standing wins the final jackpot of 10,000 diamonds.

Fairy Brawlers is a fictional mobile game developed by me, based on the successful game Brawl Stars. Following the same game dynamics, the objective is to collect as many diamonds as possible, in order to complete the chosen character's objectives (each character participates of the event for a specific reason. I have no intention of programming it or making it real, just to exercise my skills as a vis. Dev and art director.)
